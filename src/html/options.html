Important Notice

Automated interactions on LinkedIn may violate their Terms of Service. Use this extension responsibly and at your own risk. To maintain natural engagement and avoid detection:

  • Start Conservative: Set comment limit to close to your organic activity level
  • Grow Gradually: Increase comment limit by 10-15% weekly if no issues
  • Stay Under Radar: Consistency beats volume spikes

Example: Normally 5/day? Start with 6-7, not 20.
